Too excited to sleep. Can't wait to see mom and dad. Can't wait to see Kate and Meg.

Had an awkward goodbye with Toby yesterday but "hugs" aren't really forte. Toby is such a sensitive and motivated person. He is drive. To do many things in life and I am so glad to have met him and been able to learn some things with him. A poem that he gave me, which I have now memorized is a kind of mantra for a life well lived - take what you want from it:

Be good, and if god wills it so be great.
The first is all in all, the last must wait,
Till time awards the final estimate

"I ought" will lead you Higher than "I would"
And s lo it is, how rarely understood
Good to be great but greater to be good
